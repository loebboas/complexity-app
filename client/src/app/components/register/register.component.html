 Tutorial I

We really planned to to a Tutorial here, as well as other stuff we couldn't finish in time. But, here is the main idea:

  • Wherever you are, in our infinite Cloud of Complexity (not yet though): The Complexity App is basically one infinite Network. Wherever you are, you can create new multidimensional Objects, which we call "Thoughts".
  • Thoughts can usually be found in the context (or as the content) of another Thought. Together, they constitute a theoretically infinite network of all kinds of information.
  • Thoughts though, are much more complex. They are connected through several other dimensions as well. This makes it very difficult to imagine complex networks. This is where our App comes in.
Tutorial II

Our app lets you create infinite complex networks, without ever having to think more than few connections at a time. You can create your own networks and add Dimensions for each of them. Our App helps you to navigate and understand such complex networks. Instead of trying to understand multidimensional Networks, we let you navigate through different Dimensions, by creating your own perspectives. In short:

Create New Thoughts and add as many Dimensions as you want.
